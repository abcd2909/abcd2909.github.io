Some Rhythm Games Recommendation (mobile)




This website will introduce you to some rhythm games that I have played or seen before,

***and those games that I have not listed do not mean that they are not good.***



1.Dynamix



Introduction： Dynamix, the hardcore mobile music game is the first publishing game made by C4Cat. Dynamix introduces a new style of music game. Its triple-dropping track design provides a more rhythmical and varying play style. Players can feel like composing the song during gameplay. The first release version of Dynamix includes more than 20 Hong Kong original songs.

Game play (Easy) Game play (crazy)



platform：ios 、android

mode：solo

difficulty：★ ~ ★★★★★

rating：★★★★☆ (Because I think this game is not only suitable for novices, but also for veterans to challenge themselves.Besides, the music is pretty good. So I give it 4.5 stars)

download link：download from google play

2.Arcaea



Introduction： Made by Lowiro Limited. Arcaea is an arcade-style rhythm game where players must touch, hold slide to the beat of the music. Arcaea has a really high difficulty where experience growth is a factor in the game, with over 90 songs to try that features more than 50 artists from around the globe and each song has three difficulty levels to master, it will give any players a whole new experience with the different music genre to try, and they can expect new music through content updates. You can challenge your friends on the online leaderboards to see who has the highest score. If you are in it for the challenge, then this is one of the best mobile games with high difficulty.

Game play (Easy) Game play (crazy)



platform：ios 、android、Nintendo Switch

mode：solo

difficulty：★★ ~ ★★★★☆

rating：★★★★☆ ( Good story, good music, unique gameplay, and beautiful game interface. 4.5 stars)

download link：download from google play
